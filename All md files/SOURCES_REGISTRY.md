## 📚 PRIMARY SOURCES

### 1. Sefaria API - https://www.sefaria.org/api
The primary online Jewish text library containing over 100+ halachic references.

#### **Halakhic Codifiers & Codes**

**Shulchan Arukh (Code of Jewish Law) - Rabbi Yosef Karo (1565)**
- **Orach Chaim** (Laws of Daily Life) - 900 sections
  - Prayer laws (sections 89-102)
  - Shabbat laws (sections 242-321)
  - Holiday laws (sections 429-689)
  - Fast days (sections 550-570)
  - Blessings (sections 204-230)
  
- **Yoreh De'ah** (Laws of Prohibited Foods) - 384 sections
  - Kashrut (dietary laws) - sections 87-110
  - Shechita (ritual slaughter) - sections 1-28
  - Niddah (family purity) - sections 183-200
  - Cherem (excommunication) - sections 334-346
  
- **Even HaEzer** (Laws of Marriage & Divorce) - 178 sections
  - Marriage (sections 26-80)
  - Divorce (sections 119-154)
  - Ketubah (marriage contract) (sections 66-78)
  
- **Choshen Mishpat** (Laws of Civil Law) - 427 sections
  - Business law (section 183)
  - Theft (section 348)
  - Honest weights (section 228)

**Mishnah Berurah - Rabbi Israel Meir HaCohen (1938)**
- Authoritative Ashkenazi commentary on Shulchan Arukh Orach Chaim
- 35 melachot (prohibited creative acts on Shabbat)
- Referenced extensively for Shabbat law (sections 242+)

**Rambam (Maimonides) - Mishneh Torah (1180)**
- 14-book systematic code of Jewish law
- Laws of Shabbat (Sefer Zemanim)
- Laws of Prayer (Sefer Ahava)
- Laws of Forbidden Foods (Sefer Kedusha)
- Laws of Chametz and Matzah (Sefer Zemanim)

**Rama (Rabbi Moshe Isserles) - Shulchan Arukh Glosses (1577)**
- Ashkenazi custom addenda to Shulchan Arukh
- Major authority for Ashkenazi practice

**Aruch HaShulchan - Rabbi Yechiel Mikhel Epstein (1908)**
- Comprehensive Ashkenazi commentary
- Bridges earlier and later authorities

**Chayei Adam & Chochmat Adam - Rabbi Abraham Danzig (1812)**
- Concise guide to practical halakha
- Ashkenazi custom-focused

#### **Talmudic Sources**

**Talmud Bavli (Babylonian Talmud)**
- Primary Jewish legal source used throughout
- 63 tractates covering all areas of law

**Talmud Yerushalmi (Jerusalem Talmud)**
- Alternative talmudic tradition
- 39 tractates

#### **Rishonim (Medieval Commentators)**

**Tosafot** - Medieval commentaries on Talmud  
**Rosh (Rabbi Asher ben Yechiel)** - Talmudic decisions  
**Mordechai (Rabbi Mordechai ben Hillel)** - Talmudic rulings  
**Tur (Rabbi Yakov ben Asher)** - 4-part halakhic code (precursor to Shulchan Arukh)

#### **Midrashic & Early Sources**

**Midrash Rabbah** - Homiletic interpretations of Torah and Megillot  
**Mekhilta** - Talmudic interpretation of Exodus

#### **Holiday & Liturgical Texts**

**Pesach Haggadah** - Passover order of service with liturgical texts  
**Yom Kippur** - Dedicated texts on Day of Atonement laws  
**Rosh Hashanah** - New Year laws and customs  
**Full Tanakh** (Hebrew Bible) - Torah, Prophets, Writings

#### **Modern Authorities (Referenced)**

**Rav Moshe Feinstein** - 20th-century American Ashkenazi decisor  
**Rav Yaakov Kamenetsky** - American Yeshiva head  
**Rav Shlomo Zalman Auerbach** - Jerusalem posek  
**Rav Yosef Shalom Elyashiv** - Jerusalem halakhic authority

### 4. Community Customs Database

14 comprehensive community traditions with specific minhagim (customs) and halachic practices.

#### **Communities & Sources**

**Ashkenazi Jews**
- File: `customs/ashkenaz.json`
- Origin: Central & Eastern Europe
- Primary authorities: Rema, Mishnah Berurah, Aruch HaShulchan
- Coverage: 50+ halachic topics

**Sefardi Jews**
- File: `customs/sefardic.json`
- Origin: Mediterranean & North Africa
- Primary authorities: Shulchan Arukh (Rav Yosef Karo), Ben Ish Chai, Kaf HaChaim
- Key practice: Permit kitniyot (legumes) on Passover

**Yemenite Jews**
- File: `customs/yemenite.json`
- Origin: Yemen
- Distinctive practices: Unique prayer nusach, liturgical variations
- Key source: Yemenite community traditions

**Bukharian Jews**
- File: `customs/bukharian.json`
- Origin: Uzbekistan & Central Asia
- Authorities: Ben Ish Chai, Kaf HaChaim (Sephardic authorities)
- Coverage: Kashrut, Pesach, Shabbat customs

**Mizrahi Jews**
- File: `customs/persian.json`, `customs/iraqi.json`
- Origin: Iran, Iraq, and Eastern Mediterranean
- Halachic tradition: Primarily Sephardic
- Notable: Strong Kabbalistic influences

**Kafkazi/Mountain Jews** (`customs/mountain-jewish:kavkazi.json`)
- Origin: Caucasus Mountains
- Heritage: Persian-influenced traditions
- Unique customs: Govgil (end of Pesach community celebration)
- Authorities: Edot HaMizrach, local community rabbinate

**Ethiopian Jews (Beta Israel)**
- File: `customs/ethiopian.json`
- Origin: Ethiopia
- Distinctive feature: Ancient traditions predating modern diaspora

**Georgian Jews**
- File: `customs/georgian.json`
- Origin: Georgia (Caucasus)
- Traditions: Unique liturgical customs

**Greek/Romaniote Jews**
- File: `customs/greek-romaniote.json`
- Origin: Greece and Ottoman Empire
- Current centers: Thessaloniki and Athens

**Iraqi Jews**
- File: `customs/iraqi.json`
- Origin: Iraq (ancient Babylonia)
- Historical significance: Home of Babylonian Talmud traditions

**Moroccan Jews**
- File: `customs/moroccan.json`
- Origin: Morocco & North Africa
- Traditions: Sephardic influenced with local variations

**Persian Jews**
- File: `customs/persian.json`
- Origin: Iran
- Authorities: Sephardic halakhic tradition
- Notable: Strong Kabbalistic influence

**Syrian Jews**
- File: `customs/syrian.json`
- Origin: Syria & Levant
- Traditions: Mediterranean & Middle Eastern customs

**Turkish/Ottoman Sefardi Jews**
- File: `customs/turkish:ottoman-sefardic.json`
- Origin: Ottoman Empire & Turkey
- Heritage: Ottoman Sefardi tradition
- Authorities: Sephardic halakhic sources

## 🔗 API ENDPOINTS & DATA STRUCTURES

### Sefaria API
- Base URL: https://www.sefaria.org/api
- Endpoints:
  - `/texts/{reference}` - Fetch text with Hebrew & English
  - `/index` - Library category tree
  - `/search` - Full-text search

### Merkava API (Referenced)
- Base URL: https://www.merkava.com/api
- Endpoints:
  - `/halacha/{topic}` - Halachic rulings
  - `/customs/{community}` - Community customs

### Hebcal API
- Base URL: https://www.hebcal.com
- Endpoints:
  - `/hebcal?cfg=json` - Calendar with holidays & learning

### Halachipedia MediaWiki API
- Base URL: https://halachipedia.com/api.php
- Endpoints:
  - `?action=query&list=search` - Article search
  - `?action=query&prop=extracts` - Article content
