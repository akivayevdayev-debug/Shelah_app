Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026 · Last updated: June 2026

← Back to Sh'elah

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: Sh'elah does not sell your personal data. We collect only what we need to provide the Service and improve it. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address via our authentication provider (Clerk). We do not store your password directly — it is managed securely by Clerk.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service:

  • Questions asked and AI responses received
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Timestamps of activity
  • Bookmarks, notes, and saved preferences

Preferences

We store your user preferences to personalize your experience, including: language (English/Hebrew), theme (light/dark), community/tradition settings, and calendar display preferences.

Device & Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the Service:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Referring URLs

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies (including browser localStorage, which we use for theme/language preferences and is never transmitted to our servers):

  • Necessary cookies: Authentication session, location preferences — required for core functionality
  • Analytics: Vercel Analytics (aggregated, non-personally identifiable page views)

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings; necessary cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core sign-in functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Personalize your experience (show relevant content, remember preferences)
  • Understand usage patterns to improve features (using aggregated/anonymized data)
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Send important service announcements (not marketing emails without your opt-in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your questions or answers to train AI models without your explicit consent.

3. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Account information (name, email) Until account deletion or 1 year of inactivity
Query logs (questions + AI answers) 90 days, then anonymized
Cached AI responses 24 hours (shared across users, not linked to you)
User preferences & bookmarks Until deleted by user or account deletion
Session cookies Deleted on logout
Analytics cookies Up to 1 year
Security logs (IP, access logs) 30 days

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We share data with the following service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Clerk (clerk.com) — Authentication. Stores your name, email, and handles login security.
  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Database hosting. Stores your preferences, bookmarks, and query history.
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — Application hosting and aggregated analytics.
  • Google (Google Gemini API) — Your questions are sent to Google's AI model to generate answers. No personally identifying information is sent beyond the question text.
  • Anthropic — Fallback AI model. Same conditions as Google above.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (no personal information) for academic or halachic research purposes.

We will disclose your data if required by law (e.g., subpoena, court order), or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person or to prevent illegal activity.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Opt-out: Disable non-essential analytics cookies; unsubscribe from service emails
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes

To exercise any of these rights, email akiva.yevda@gmail.com with your account email and the specific request. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Passwords are hashed and salted by Clerk — we never see your password
  • Database data is encrypted at rest (Supabase default encryption)
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel
  • We conduct regular security reviews

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy here with an updated effective date, and for material changes we will provide a more prominent notice.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or to report a concern:

  • Email: akiva.yevda@gmail.com

This policy is provided for transparency about our data practices. It is not a substitute for independent legal advice in your jurisdiction.
